The Story

Why Did I Build
Mini Tools?

My first semester at university began, and while I was not expert with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I had great experience with WordPress and Elementor. I understood design concepts, which made the design aspect easier. To gain practical coding experience, I started this Mini Tools project, building each tool to simultaneously learn HTML, CSS, and JavaScript hands-on.
